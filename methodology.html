Framework

A process for deciding whether a move is becoming durable

I use the same framework across stocks, sectors, and broader market themes. The point is not to force every idea into a rigid checklist. It is to keep my thinking honest, legible, and anchored to what the market is actually doing.

Practical outcome

The framework helps me distinguish exciting stories from durable trends, and good ideas from poorly timed entries.
